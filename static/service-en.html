SERVICE:
OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE
Fitvend operates over 100 proprietary protein vending machines in Moscow fitness clubs; the number of our machines is growing weekly.
We have our own fully staffed storage facility and a technical service that includes two teams:
FIELD SERVICE GROUP:
Services our vending machines in fitness clubs works 24/7. On top of regular maintenance, in case of any malfunction, our technicians arrive within two hours after the call to fix the technical failure and restore uninterrupted operation.
OPERATOR TEAM:
A team of qualified IT professionals monitors the vending machines operation remotely seven days a week, eliminating any software or payment terminal related issues. Additionally, our operator team deploys regular software updates for vending machines and payment terminals.
All technical service staff undergo testing of their professional skills and regular training.
All of our devices have our customer service number «8-800» for consumers to contact with any issue related to the devices or cocktails, as well as a unique QR code for written contact with the customer service via the WhatsApp. Our online customer service makes a refund in the event of a failure in the process of issuing a paid product.
